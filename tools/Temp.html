OOB Osgi Sanity Check
Sanity check osgi bundle version across environments after upgrade or major service pack installations.
  • Paste JSON from http://host:port/system/console/bundles/.json into the text fields. Click "Compare" to see the diff.
  • Similar bundles are displayed in white.Changed bundles version are displayed in yellow. Newly added bundles are displayed in Aqua. Deleted bundles are displayed in light red.
  • Clear them by clicking "Clear"
  • After Compare. Filter the data using select box. More Details at http://experiencedelivers.adobe.com/cemblog/en/experiencedelivers/2012/11/how_to_sanity_checkosgibundlesacrossenvirnoments.html
